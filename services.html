Our Professional Services

Comprehensive solutions for property investors and business owners

Accounting & Financial Reporting

We offer tailored accounting services for property investors and entrepreneurs who want to stay compliant, access funding, and make informed business decisions.

  • Accurate monthly/quarterly management accounts
  • IFRS for SMEs and tax-compliant annual financial statements
  • Investor-ready reports and forecasts
  • Financial analysis to improve cash flow and growth

Let us manage the numbers while you grow your property empire.

Financial Advisory & Deal Structuring

We help you unlock hidden value by structuring your property deals and investment vehicles for optimal cash flow, tax efficiency, and funding readiness.

  • Property investment strategy and goal alignment
  • Feasibility studies & ROI projections
  • Guidance on financing, refinancing, and bond readiness
  • Deal packaging and presentation for investors or banks

Book a strategy call to structure your next investment the right way.

Tax Services

As a Chartered Accountant-led firm, we know how to keep your tax affairs tidy — while helping you legally minimize tax liabilities across your portfolio or business.

  • SARS registrations and annual submissions
  • Capital gains tax & VAT planning on property transactions
  • Personal, business, and trust tax optimization
  • Advice on holding structures and estate planning

Let us simplify your tax so you can focus on building wealth.

Property Management Services

We offer end-to-end property management for landlords, student accommodation providers, and investor portfolios — with a sharp focus on cash flow and tenant retention.

  • Rent collection and arrears management
  • Lease agreements, renewals, and legal compliance
  • Monthly reporting and maintenance oversight
  • Professional liaison with students and parents (for student housing)

Protect your property income with professional management.

Company Secretarial & Compliance

We handle the behind-the-scenes work to ensure your company stays compliant and well-organized — whether you’re scaling property acquisitions or pitching to banks.

  • CIPC company registrations & amendments
  • Annual returns and company secretary support
  • Shareholding structure planning & documentation
  • BEE affidavits and board resolution drafting

Stay investor-ready with seamless corporate compliance.

Legal Assistance & Risk Management

We provide access to legal support and strategic risk management solutions tailored to property investors, entrepreneurs, and developers navigating South Africa’s regulatory and contractual environment.

  • Guidance on property co-ownership agreements and lease drafting
  • Legal vetting of investor agreements, JV contracts, and OTPs
  • Risk assessments on property acquisitions and financing structures
